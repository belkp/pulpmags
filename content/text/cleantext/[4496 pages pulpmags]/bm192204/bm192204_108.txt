A Track-and-a-Half 105 "The Frog," shouted Harrison and Kelso. "Yes, the Frog," the girl repeated. "I chased after him, gave him the dope and Bill's gat and drove him out there in Red's car. I guess that's all." "Yes," said the Captain, touching a bell, "I guess that is all." The door opened and a uniformed officer led in the slinking form of Frog Fagin, outcast even of crooks, who had had his big chance and failed as usual. The telephone bell rang and Harbin answered it. It was a woman's voice. "Have you asked June Jennings yet?" it queried tauntingly.
