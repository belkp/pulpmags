"What did Larry want when he called you aside?" Mrs. Huston in- quired with more than ordinary in- terest. "It was nothing important, mother," Thelma answered indiffer- ently as she tossed an empty box into a corner of the compartment. "He wanted to come along and marry me at the first stop."
