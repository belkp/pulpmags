IT HAPPENED IN A BEAUTY PARLOR By EVA GASTON "Is this the Crescent Beauty Parlor?" Sitting at her office desk, temporarily forgetting the rows of typewriter keys which faced her, Doris Steele spoke into the telephone. The reply must have been in the affirm- ative, because her next words were: "Will you ask Nelly if she can take *10 for a shampoo and a wave at four o'clock? . . . This is Miss Steele speaking . . . yes, that's right, Steele. . . . S-t-e-e-l-e!"
