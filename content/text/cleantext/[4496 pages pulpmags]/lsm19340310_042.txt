Going Primitive 35 All night long they traveled up and down that ice flow. Several times she came close to going to sleep on her feet. Each time, Tommy shook her awake. "You can't go to sleep. If you do, you'll never wake up." Tommy did not go close to the edge when they were near him. She was glad he didn't. While it was still dark, the fog lifted, and it grew bitter, searing cold.
